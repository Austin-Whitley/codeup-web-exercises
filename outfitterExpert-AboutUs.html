Who We Are

OutfitterExpert is a free hunting and fishing online travel booking marketplace. By designing a seamless and user friendly platform for Guides, Property Owners and Fishing Charters to advertise their professional services, we have created the ultimate one stop shop for Outdoorsman to book their next adventure.

All users will benefit from our easy to use, consolidated platform due to its ability to sort and filter by region, species, trip price, etc. This ensures that all desired trips are easy to advertise, search, compare and book.

OutfitterExpert makes hunting and fishing travel booking effective, approachable and convenient for all users.

Our Purpose

OutfitterExpert is committed to revolutionizing the way Outdoorsman book travel by simplifying the Outfitter service industries advertisement and booking process.

We know first hand that finding and booking the perfect hunting or fishing trip is next to impossible. This is due to the fact that this service industry is remote, boundless and has yet to be consolidated. Guides, Property Owners and Fishing Charters are not in the business of advertising and travel booking. Outfitters provide a specific niche sought after by millions of outdoorsmen and have no effective outlet to reach their audience.

OutfitterExpert is here to solve this ancient issue. We want to remove the headache of advertising and booking for the Outfitters by providing a user friendly platform that effectively advertises their services and keeps track of their upcoming bookings.

Because OutfitterExpert is a world class free booking platform it will provide the perfect one stop shop for Outdoorsman to sort and filter through thousands of hunting and fishing trips to confidently book their next adventure.
